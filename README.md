About The Company
Our client is a rapidly growing, European scale-up in the adtech space, continually adding to their product suite.  Operating successfully across the US and Europe, they are now looking to add an in-app monetisation product to their tech stack.
 
About The Role
We are looking for someone to be one of the first members of the team, with responsibility for the delivery of the MVP for the new product. You'll be looking at:
Scoping the architecture for the in-app platform 
Delivering the early minimum viable product
Scaling the MVP to enterprise level
Managing the architecture costs versus stability and performance
Liaising with the product lead to ensure alignment on product direction
Managing OPEX and scaling the initial minimum team required
Ensuring correct engineering and governance principles are implemented
Hiring your team
The role will be dynamic and wear different hats during the early stages, until you have scaled your team.
 
About You
You will be super comfortable in start-ups and adaptable to a fluid role.  
You will have solid experience in:
Adtech start-ups, especially mobile DSPs, SSPs, monetisation platforms, etc
Solid background in cloud, data, back and front end architecture
Experience building SDKs
Deep exposure to RTB, prebid, programmatic etc
Mobile gaming sector knowledge
If you're interested in being part of a success story from its inception, this role is for you.
